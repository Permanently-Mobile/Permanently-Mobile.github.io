Questions

Frequently Asked

What does "self-hosted" mean? ▼
PM Books runs on your own machine or server. Your financial data never touches a cloud service. You install it, you run it, you control it. Works on any system with Node.js 20+.
How does the AI work without sending data to the cloud? ▼
PM Books uses Venice AI, a zero-data-retention provider. Your prompts are processed and immediately discarded. Nothing is stored, nothing is used for training. You can also use local AI models if you prefer fully offline operation.
Do I need accounting knowledge to use this? ▼
No. The setup wizard generates everything based on your business type. The AI assistant handles categorization and account mapping. You just describe expenses in plain English and PM Books does the rest.
Can I use this for my taxes? ▼
Yes. The Pro tier includes Schedule C export and tax-ready reports. Every transaction maps to an IRS category. At tax time, export everything your accountant needs in one click. This is bookkeeping software, not tax filing software, but it gives your accountant exactly what they need.
Is there a monthly fee? ▼
You choose: one-time payment or monthly subscription. Either way, you get the same software. The one-time price is a better deal if you plan to use it for more than a few months. No usage limits beyond your tier's AI and OCR quotas.
What about the Telegram bot? ▼
The Pro tier includes a Telegram bot that lets you log expenses, check balances, and ask questions from your phone. Send a message like "lunch $12 at Chipotle" and it creates a categorized transaction instantly. Receipts can be sent as photos directly in the chat.
